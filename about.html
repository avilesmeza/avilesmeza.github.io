About

Edvard Aviles-Meza

I am an Assistant Professor of Philosophy at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. I received my PhD in Philosophy, with a concentration in Cognitive Science, from Cornell University.

Before that, I earned a BA in Psychology and completed two master’s degrees: one in Neuroscience at UNMSM and another in Philosophy at Central European University (CEU).

My research lies primarily in philosophy of mind and epistemology, approached through the lens of cognitive science.

Outside philosophy, I enjoy playing and composing music, as well as filmmaking—interests I try to bring together through documentary work. I also love mixed martial arts (MMA), especially striking.

I was born and raised in Lima, Perú.
