Areas of Research

Research

01

Consciousness and Attention

I study the relationship between attention and conscious experience, including introspective access, distraction, perceptual overflow, and the temporal structure of awareness.

02

Mental Representation and Cognitive Maps

I investigate the formats of mental representation, with particular interest in spatial representation, cognitive maps, and map-like neural codes.

03

Natural and Artificial Intelligence

My newer work examines the foundations of a theory of intelligence and the relations among natural cognition, psychometrics, biological systems, and artificial intelligence.

04

Epistemology and Cognitive Science

I approach questions about knowledge, introspection, and rationality through the empirical study of perception, attention, imagination, and cognition.
